Story

Humpty Dum`pty is a popular nursery rhyme that tells the tale of an egg called Humpty Dumpty. As the name implies, Humpty Dumpty sits on a wall and fell off of it and then nobody could help him - thus the lines "All the kings horses and all the kings men couldn't put humpty together again". Dumpty might be a onomatopeia but it also is a type of ale... Humpty might've been drunk.

Adaptions

In CocoMelon, Humpty Dumpty is an egg that comes from their refigerator. Their "Physics teacher", the Bear, had told them to do a science experiment involving dropping Humpty off the deck, and preventing him from cracking. When each of their ideas failed, they worked together and upgraded the last egg so that it did not break.

Possible "Moral-of-the-Story"

According to Merriam-webster, something that once got damaged can never be repaired or made operative again (like Humpty Dumpty, broken once, never fixed).
